A Deep Lurking Thing (Muckie)
Dir. David Parkinson --- DP: Dylan Looney
A Short Documentary
"A Deep Lurking Thing: A Short Film About A Short Film About The Muckross Lake Monster "

In 2018, David Parkinson directed and presented a documentary about "Muckie", the Muckross Lake Monster. In this short film, he revisits the film, through reconstructing its original footage - and adding some of his own and some from elsewhere - in an attempt to decipher whether or not it revealed anything.
