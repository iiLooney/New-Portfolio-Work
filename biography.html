Biography

Dylan Looney is a camera and visuals oriented individual, currently studying at Screen and Film School Brighton. Having taken on the role of Director of Photography on numerous short films and music videos, Dylan feels confident and competent with crafting the visual aesthetic of a piece. Some of this work can be seen in the "Work" tab above, with his most recent short film being Cohuleen Driuth, a film about xyz.

Dylan has also ventured into the world of the colourist on numerous occasions, grading over five short films to a professional standard. He has been grading clips on DaVinci Resolve for over 4 years and feels confident in acquiring the client's intended look on each and every project,

Becoming competent within a range of skills is something Dylan relishes, most recently learning web development in order to personally make this website to his vision! Once graduated, Dylan is excited to kick-start his career in the industry by working freelance in trainee roles, as well as shooting more short films on the side to continue working on his craft.
