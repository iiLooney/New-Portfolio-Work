Dylan Looney is an independent, freelance colourist based in Brighton, England.
Originally from Ireland, Dylan has a great passion for the visual image and the data and skill that goes into crafting the final look.

Being an intuitive, detail-driven individual, Dylan has found his niche working as a DIT on TV productions and love the organisational and creative workflows involved within the role. He aims to learn more about the science and data involved in capturing the visual image, and how he can use this knowledge to create the best final image.

As an aspiring on-set dit/colourist, Dylan endeavours to continue to find new, exciting opportunities to work within these roles in order to become the most reliable and trusting collaborator he can possibly be.
