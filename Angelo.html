A Short Documentary
Angelo, a young boy suffering at the hands of bullies, speaks to Jamie, a man who has overcome a debilitating speech impediment, for advice on how to overcome and persevere through his own difficulties.

"What an amazing experience it was meeting Angelo, a very very brave young man who is dealing with bullies. Individuals who bully people will never achieve anything in life, not then, not now, and certainly not in the future." - Jamie Googan
